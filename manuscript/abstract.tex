This manuscript under review is organized around one direct object,
\[
\Phi(s):=\Gamma(s)(1-s)\zeta(s),\qquad s\in\C.
\]
In the strip $0<\RePart(s)<1$, the correspondence
\[
\Phi(s)=0\iff\zeta(s)=0
\]
is immediate from nonvanishing of $\Gamma(s)$ and $1-s$.
The core unresolved theorem target is the critical-line exclusion implication
\[
\Phi(s)=0\Longrightarrow \RePart(s)=\tfrac12
\quad (0<\RePart(s)<1).
\]
If that implication is proved, RH follows by direct reduction.
The manuscript is presented as a proposed proof framework and materials for mathematical audit; it does not claim this core implication is currently proved.
